RIYO Point India Pvt Ltd

Indian National Highways Brought More Closure.

RIYO Rent It Over India Pvt. Ltd. To solve every highway problem and optimize highway experiences across India. RIYO provides all logistic services under one roof, accessible to all highway entities. It is a new and revolutionary idea that works on simplifying the national highway, but Webreate played a critical role in giving this idea new life. In this digital economy, no idea is successful unless it has a digital presence. Webreate played an essential role in giving this idea a digital life. Webreate handled everything from branding to development.
It was always critical for us that a company like Rent It Over India Pvt. Ltd. obtains the most favourable online footprints. The goal was to create a seamless interactive website that conveyed the business message and worked in accordance with the organization's mission and vision. The motive of website was to know users about the business and second motive was to create a user interactive app that can help users to book the services of RIYO. Encouraging more users to use mobile app.

PROBLEM

No business is successful unless it encounters problems, and developing a seamless and responsive website and the app is always a new challenge. The business concept was the first in the segment because it primarily aims to bring logistics services under one roof. Creating a website for such a revolutionary idea is a difficult task in and of itself.
The main issue was determining how to categorize client services and integrate the business's service packages. Because we need to launch the website and app within the 4 months of time. We had to work according to the client's specifications and keep him updated about the task.

CHALLENGING

Without customers, no business can exist. RIYO India primarily serves highway entities and truck drivers, so developing an app for them was a difficult task. We needed to create a very simple, seamless and user-friendly app for truck drivers. It was our task to provide them with the most simple user-friendly app, and integrating all RIYO services without changing the programming of the app and website was difficult. 
• The most difficult challenge was making the app and website responsive in areas with limited internet coverage. RIYO India serves all of India, so we needed to create a digital friendly ecosystem. 
• It was a very challenging task for us to learn about the use cases of the app and website in accordance to the end user.
• It wasn't easy to create a website and app, but targeting the right audience online was more challenging. Knowing the right audience to brand the company was not only a challenge but also a requirement of the client.

SOLUTION

Overcoming business challenges is one of the most satisfying tasks in the business world. Challenges are everywhere, but how we overcome them is what matters. It was essential for us that RIYO to be perceived as something genuine and authentic business. It was vital for us to ensure delivery of the project on time.
First, the developers were assigned to create a responsive and user-friendly website. Our team members used the best data they could gather during the business analysis and research through various social media platforms and interaction with highway drivers to create a user-friendly website and app. 
  We were aware of the intended audience after performing extensive business research. So, we focused on developing a very simple and user-friendly app, integrating all of the services and packages in the app with the assistance of developers to make the app very light to use. A trustworthy and realistic representation of the user persona was created for the users. A use case for the app was defined; this was a critical step in determining how the app would function and how the interface would appear.
  We also created interactive Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn pages and a YouTube channel to increase brand awareness.   A digital ecosystem was created to match the RIYO Mission and Vision.
