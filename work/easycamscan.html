EASY CAMSCAN

A Scanning App, the most important tool for your Documentation

Easy Cam Scan is a pdf doc scanner mobile scanning tool designed by flyfott solutions mainly for scanning essential documents and papers and provides a Simple and Fast UI to work efficiently. The goal was to make scanning of hard copies into pdfs easier for people, specifically businessmen and students. A Concept was carried out with a very different theme. The concept was to get all the necessary and important documents in a single place on your smartphone.
EASY CAM SCAN is an application with advanced editing tools where you can store all your hard copy documents and essential papers in the form of a pdf and automatically save it on your smartphone just by quick scanning the essential documents.

PROBLEM

The Main problem we faced was increasing the App visibility by encouraging more consumers to use the mobile App by increasing brand awareness and boosting its ranking by popularizing this App across all social media platforms through social media posts and reels in order to spread awareness about this App.
The important thing was making effective smart ads and creating indulging social media posts to help in targeting a large audience that is highly likely to download and use the App. We had to make sure that our posts and videos on social medias are compelling and engaging.
